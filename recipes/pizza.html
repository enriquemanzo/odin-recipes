Pizza

Description

Hawaiian pizza is the best pizza hand's down! Here is a recipe of hawaiian pizza made with thin crust, pineapple, and ham. Try it out, you won't regret it ;)

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Pineapple Pieces
  • Turkey Pieces
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Thin Pizza Crust

Steps

  1. Start the process by going to Costco and buying thin pizza crust
  2. Grab some canned tomatoe sauce and apply that bad boy to the crust
  3. Add some oregano spice to your pizza crust
  4. Apply your mozzarella cheese on the pizza crust
  5. Open a can of pineapple and generously add pineapple to your pizza crust
  6. Generously add turkey pieces to your pizza
  7. Place your pizza on the oven and turn up the temperature up to 1000 degree Celsius
  8. Your pizza should be done after 3 minutes
