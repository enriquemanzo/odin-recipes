English Muffin

Description

This bad boy right here is a McMuffin. Arguably the best breakfast sandwich worldwide.Not much else to say other than go to McDonald's and get yourself one.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • English Muffin Bread
  • Sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. First, place the bread in the oven at 100 degree Celsius and heat that thang up for 10 minutes
  2. Microwave your sausage and cheese together on a plate for 10 seconds
  3. Place your egg in the microwave for 30 seconds
  4. Place your sausage and melted cheese ingredients on your toasted bread
  5. Place you egg on the sandwich, apply ketchup, and enoy!!!
