Sandwich

Description

This sandwich is a very basic sandwich, but it's so basic that it's delicious you know? You start by toasting two slices of bread, cut a few slices of tomatoes, wash some spinach, grab a few slices of turkey meat, and cut some jalapenos. Once the ingredients have been prepared, we are ready to make our delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Turkey Meat
  • Slices of Tomatoes
  • Jalapenos
  • Spinach
  • Two Slices of Wheat Bread

Steps

  1. Place your two slices of wheat bread on a plate
  2. Add some turkey meat on one of the slices of bread
  3. Add some tomatoe slices
  4. Add your spinach
  5. Add your sliced jalapenos
  6. Add your second slice of bread and enjoy the best sandwich in the world!
